Building the Forum for Research Leadership

NDRF has been a pioneer of collaborative research in India. NDRF has set the direction for the NDRF (Consortium) by sensitizing the research ecosystem partners on the evolving technologies that shape the contemporary world, and set new directions for engineering research to address societal challenges.

NDRF DARPA Model

National Design Awards

In the view of promoting Research and advance Engineering Design activity in the Country, the National Design and Research Forum (NDRF) conducts National Design Award competition every year to identify and recognize the talent of the people those who have been making outstanding contributions in the field of Engineering Design & Research

Student Design Awards

National Design and Research Forum (NDRF) conducts annually National Level Design Competition for Students. The competition is intended to give students an opportunity to exhibit their individual engineering skills and talents and earn recognition, while promoting engineering design and research

THE NDRF CONSORTIUM




NDRF network consists of Private Industries, Government R&D Labs, Academic Institutes, Professional Societies and other stake holders including funding agencies and end-users. NDRF has Govt. R&D network of National Aerospace Laboratories (NAL), Naval Research Board (NRB), Defence Research and Development Organization (DRDO), Naval Science & Technological Laboratory (NSTL), Gas Turbine Research Establishment (GTRE), National Institute of Ocean Technology (NIOT), National Physical and Ocean Laboratory (NPOL), Aeronautical Development Establishment (ADE), Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), Defence Bioengineering and Electromedical Laboratory (DEBEL) etc., NDRF has a consortium of around 40+ Industry and Academic Institutions associated in carrying out various research activities. More than 40 MoUs have been signed between NDRF and Universities, Academic Institutions, Private R&D Laboratories, Start-Ups and Professional Societies. NDRF also planned to setup an Intellectual Property Cell in order to support its Consortium partners apart from its in-house R&D Projects. NDRF has applied for nine Patents in its name with support from IEI.
